NORTH COUNTY, Mo. (KMOV.com) - A 16-year-old was hospitalized after being shot outside a party in North County early Monday morning.
A residence in the 2100 block of Empire was hosting a party when several gunshots were heard around 2 a.m., police said.
After the gunshots were heard, the teenager ran inside with a gunshot wound to his left buttock. The injury is not believed to be life threatening.
Authorities have not released any information regarding suspects.
